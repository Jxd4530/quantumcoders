Yoga is a holistic practice that combines physical postures, breathing exercises, and meditation. The benefits of yoga include improved flexibility, increased strength, stress relief, and enhanced mental clarity. It has a rich history that dates back thousands of years in ancient India, with roots in spiritual and philosophical traditions. Practicing yoga is important for maintaining a healthy lifestyle, promoting mental well-being, and fostering a deeper connection with oneself.
